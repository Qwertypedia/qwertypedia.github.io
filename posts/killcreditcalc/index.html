Kill Credit Calculator

Usage: Each new line represents a "hit". Line 1 represents the first hit, line 2 is the second hit and so on. A hit is represented as a player and that players' dealt damage, seperated by a comma "player, damage". A player can be named anything, e.g, "1", "player1" or "Durial321".
